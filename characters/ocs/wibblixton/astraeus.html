Full In-Depth Description:

Astraeus Ichiro Takahashi, the enigmatic newcomer to Wibblix High, is a direct, tall and handsome rich guy that never asks if he can ask a question, whose presence gathers attention from his peers. From the moment he arrives in a golden helicopter, Astraeus's aura exudes an air of extravagance and luxury that nobody else has. With every gesture and display of wealth, he leaves an indelible impression on those around him.

At the heart of his persona lies his unfathomable fortune, a vast empire of riches that seemingly knows no bounds. His introduction to the school is marked not only by the grandeur of his arrival but also by his huge displays of wealth, from offering substantial sums of money for seemingly trivial tasks to unveiling a yacht worth 600 billion dollars.

His wealth is not merely a backdrop to his character but serves as a central theme that permeates every aspect of his being. His possessions, ranging from diamond-encrusted smartphones that costs trillions of dollars to a fleet of luxury vehicles, are emblematic of his extravagant lifestyle and serve as constant reminders of his exalted status. He lives in a mansion made out of gold, in the mountains, located in the small town of Wibblixton. He has golden short hair and yellow eyes, celebrates his birthday on June 15th, and he's 17 years old.

Yet, despite his immense wealth, Astraeus carries himself with an air of nonchalance and indifference, as if the trappings of fortune hold little sway over his true priorities. He is more interested in engaging with his classmates and participating in the daily activities of school life than flaunting his riches for personal gain.

His generosity truly knows no bounds, as he engages in what can be described as "giveaways" throughout the narrative, offering substantial sums of money as incentives for desired behaviors or outcomes. He already announces a 100,000,000 dollars giveaway event on his very first day at school in the middle of the class.

However, beneath his surface lies a shroud of mystery, surrounding the source of Astraeus's wealth. Despite the students' persistent inquiries, Astraeus remains elusive about the origins of his fortune, teasing tantalizing hints without fully disclosing the truth. It is only through a daring bet and a revelation about his father's asteroid mining industry that the full extent of Astraeus's financial prowess is revealed, leaving the students and anyone else astounded by the sheer magnitude of his wealth, making him the world's first quadrillionaire student, getting a monthly pay of around 62.8 trillion dollars. His dad's wealth is kept as a secret, but it is believed that he has hundreds of quadrillions if not quintillions of dollars.

Astraeus's character is not defined solely by his riches but also by his intelligence, charisma, and astute understanding of human nature. He navigates the complex social dynamics of Wibblix High with ease, captivating his classmates with his charm and wit while at the same time challenging their perceptions of his wealth and privilege.
